About The Little Pickers

A community of passionate young people — and the grown-ups who cheer them on — on a mission to make our streets sparkle!

✨ How It All Began

Our Story 📖

It started on an ordinary Tuesday afternoon. Six-year-old Ella was walking home from school when she noticed litter scattered all along her favourite path through the park. Sweet wrappers, plastic bottles, crisp packets — it made her feel sad and angry all at once.

"Why do people just throw things on the floor? Don't they know it hurts the animals?" she asked her mum. And instead of just complaining, little Ella decided to do something about it.

That weekend, armed with a pair of washing-up gloves and a carrier bag, Ella headed back to the park and started picking up litter. A neighbour stopped to help. Then a friend from school. Then their parents. Within three weeks, they had a team of ten — and The Little Pickers was born.

What started as one small girl's big idea has grown into a thriving community group. We've cleaned up over 40 streets, filled 150 bags of rubbish, and inspired more than 85 children to take pride in where they live. And it all started with Ella, aged 6, refusing to walk past litter without doing something. 💪

Our Mission 🎯

Three big goals. Hundreds of little heroes. One cleaner world.

🧹

Cleaner Streets

We want every street, park and green space in our community to be litter-free. One pick-up at a time, we're making that dream a reality for everyone who lives here.

📚

Educated Kids

Knowledge is power! We teach children about the environmental damage litter causes — to animals, water, soil and air — so they grow up making better choices for life.

🏡

Proud Communities

When people help clean up their streets, they feel ownership and pride. We want everyone in our community to walk outside and feel happy about where they live.
