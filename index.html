Our Story

One Small Voice,
One Huge Mission

It started with a simple question on a walk to school: "Why is there so much rubbish on the floor?"

That question came from a 6-year-old girl who decided she wasn't going to just wonder — she was going to do something about it. She grabbed a bag, put on her gloves, and started picking up litter from the pavement. One piece at a time.

But she didn't want to do it alone. She wanted every child in the neighbourhood, the town, the country — maybe even the world — to join in. And just like that, The Little Pickers was born.

The name? A play on "litter pickers" — because we're little, we're mighty, and we're picking up after a world that sometimes forgets to look after itself.

"If all the children pick up litter together, we can clean the whole world!"
— Our Founder, age 6

How It Works

Three Simple Steps to
Make a Difference

Every weekend, we come together as a community. No experience needed — just a willingness to help!

01

Meet Up

Join us at a local meeting point every weekend. Bring the family — everyone is welcome, from toddlers to grandparents!

02

Pick Up

Grab a litter picker, pop on some gloves, and start cleaning! We work together street by street, making it fun with games and challenges.

03

Clean Up

Celebrate what you've achieved! See the streets looking brand new, earn your Little Picker badges, and feel proud of the difference you've made.

It really is that simple. No special skills needed — just a big heart and a pair of hands! 🧤
