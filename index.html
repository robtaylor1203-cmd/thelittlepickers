What We Do

Three simple things that make a big difference to our community.

🗑️

Pick Up Litter

Every weekend we head out armed with gloves, bags and litter pickers to clean up our streets, parks and green spaces. No piece of litter is too small!

🌱

Protect Our Planet

We teach children why litter is harmful to animals, plants and waterways. When kids understand the impact, they become passionate guardians of the Earth.

🎉

Have Fun Together

Who says saving the planet can't be fun? We make every session a celebration with games, stickers, and the proud feeling of a job well done!
