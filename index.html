What We Do

Small Actions,
Enormous Impact

Every weekend, families and children come together to make their community a little bit cleaner — and in doing so, they’re changing the world.

01

Pick Up Litter

Every weekend we head out armed with gloves, bags and litter pickers to clean up our streets, parks and green spaces. No piece of litter is too small to matter.

02

Protect Our Planet

We teach children why litter is harmful to animals, plants and waterways. When children understand the impact, they become lifelong guardians of the natural world.

03

Build Community

Neighbours meet, friendships form, and children discover the profound satisfaction of making a real, visible difference to the place they call home.
