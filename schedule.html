How It Works 🤔

Coming to a session is super easy — just three simple steps!

1
📅

Check the Schedule

Browse our upcoming sessions below. Find one near you on a day that works — we run sessions most weekends throughout the year!

2
👟

Come Along!

No need to book in advance (unless a session is marked as Full). Just show up at the meeting point at 10:00 AM. Bring the family, bring friends!

3
🎉

Pick & Celebrate!

We'll kit you out, have a safety briefing, then get picking! Sessions finish at 12:00 PM. We count the bags, cheer, and feel amazing about what we've done! 🏆

What to Bring 🎒

We provide litter pickers, bags and hi-vis vests — just bring these essentials and you're good to go!

🧤

Gloves

We provide disposable ones, but your own rubber gloves are even better!

🦾

Litter Picker

We have plenty to lend, but if you have your own bring it along!

🛍️

Bags

We supply biodegradable bags at every session — no need to bring your own.

💧

Water Bottle

Stay hydrated! Bring a refillable water bottle to keep you going.

👕

Old Clothes

Wear something you don't mind getting muddy — it's all part of the fun!

👟

Sturdy Shoes

Trainers or wellies depending on the weather. No flip-flops please!

☀️

Sun Cream

In summer months, protect those noses! Especially important for little ones.

😊

A Big Smile!

The most important thing of all. Come ready to have fun and be proud! 🌟
